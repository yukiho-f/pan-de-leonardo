Concepto

Concept

“Pan de Leonardo” means “Breads of Leonardo” in Spanish. The owner, Leonardo decided to start this bakery based on the concept “Bringing a little taste of Chile to Japan.”

Not only is Pan de Leonardo a Chilean bakery , but also base for a Hispanic community. It can be tough to live in a foreign country, so this bakery aims for being a community which all Hispanics can feel at home in.

Productos

Products

Regular items

Empanada

Baked bread filled with ground beef, onion, boiled egg, and olive.

Marraqueta

Bread made with flour, salt, water, and yeast, similar to French bread.

Tres leches cake

Butter cake soaked in 3 kinds of milk (evaporated milk, condensed milk, and heavy cream).

Hallulla

Flat round baked bread which has similar texture to scone. Often served with Pebre.

Empolvados

Pastry filled with manjar (dulce de leche) and covered with powdered sugar.

Berlín

Austro-German origin pastry filled with custard cream.
